1. Work Hours
- Core collaboration hours: 10:00 AM–3:00 PM local time.
- Flexible hours outside core hours are allowed with manager approval.

2. Paid Time Off (PTO)
- Full-time employees accrue 1.5 days PTO per month.
- PTO requests should be submitted at least 5 business days in advance.
- Sick time: notify your manager as soon as possible; no minimum notice required.

3. Remote Work
- Remote work is allowed up to 3 days/week by default.
- Fully remote roles require written approval from HR and the hiring manager.
- Employees must maintain a reliable internet connection and a quiet workspace for meetings.

4. Security and Access
- Do not share account credentials.
- Use MFA for all company accounts.
- Lost device must be reported to IT within 24 hours.

5. Expense Policy (High Level)
- Business expenses require itemized receipts.
- Reimbursements are processed every Friday.
- Meals are reimbursable up to $35/day during travel.

FAQ
Q: Can I carry over PTO to next year?
A: Up to 5 unused PTO days may be carried over into the next calendar year.

Q: How do I request remote work beyond 3 days/week?
A: Submit a Remote Work Exception form; HR and your manager must approve.
