Product FAQ (Sample Knowledge Base)
Product Name: Acme Notes
Last Updated: 2026-02-01

Getting Started
- Acme Notes is a note-taking app that supports tags, folders, and offline mode.
- Offline mode syncs automatically when the device reconnects to the internet.

Accounts and Billing
- Free plan: up to 50 notes, 3 folders, and 1 device.
- Pro plan: unlimited notes, unlimited folders, up to 5 devices.
- Team plan: shared workspaces, admin controls, SSO support.

Common Questions
Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Go to Settings → Account → Reset Password. You will receive a reset email.

Q: Does Acme Notes support Markdown?
A: Yes. Toggle Markdown in Settings → Editor.

Q: Why is my sync failing?
A: Common causes:
  1) You are offline.
  2) Your token expired; log out and log back in.
  3) Your workspace is over storage limit.

Troubleshooting Checklist
- Check your network connection.
- Confirm the app version is 2.4.0 or later.
- Try signing out and signing back in.
- If still failing, contact support with your user ID and device model.
